How it works

How does ebin° works?

  • Sell Feature
    • 1) Users would be able to login onto the app via Facebook, Google, or Apple.
    2) From the home screen, users can select the Sell Button in the category.
    3) Users are given the option to select the Brand and Model of their phones.
    4) Users will be asked a set of questions about the current state of their phones.
    5) Users would need to upload a picture of their phones for our verification process.
    6) Users would be asked for their IMEI and Serial Number of the phone for our verification.
    7) Users are given a guaranteed price of their phones if the phone matches the criteria information.
    8) Users would need to enter their shipping information for their Phone Kit.
    9) Place your order to Checkout, and verify the information before placing your order.
  • Buy Feature
    • 1) Users would be able to login onto the app via Facebook, Google, or Apple.
    2) From the home screen, users can select the Buy Button in the category.
    3) Users are given the option to select the Brand and Model of their phones.
    4) Users will be asked a set of questions about the current state of their phones.
    5) Users would need to upload a picture of their phones for our verification process.
    6) Users would be asked for their IMEI and Serial Number of the phone for our verification.
    7) Users are given a guaranteed price of their phones if the phone matches the criteria information.
    8) Users would need to enter their shipping information for their Phone Kit.
  • Repair Feature
    • 1) Users would be able to login onto the app via Facebook, Google, or Apple.
    2) From the home screen, users can select the Repair Button in the category.
    3) Users are given the option to select the Brand and Model of their phones.
    4) Users will be asked a set of questions about the type of repair and about the current state of their phones.
    5) Users would need to have access to their IMEI Number and Serial Number of their phones for our verification.
    6) Users have the option to Bring In For Repair or Ship In For Repair to our ebin Certified* repair shops.
    7) Users would be prompted with a list of our ebin Certified* repair shops and asked to proceed through our booking process.
    8) Users have completed their reservation for their upcoming appointment to our ebin Certified* repair shops.
