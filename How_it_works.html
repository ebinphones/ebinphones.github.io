Sell Feature Details

      Before You Begin:
      Back Up Data: Backup any important data or files from your device to ensure you don't lose any information during the sell process.
      Factory Reset Your Phone: To protect your privacy and ensure that the next owner starts with a clean slate, perform a factory reset on your phone. A factory reset will erase all data on your device and restore it to its original settings. To do this, go to your device's settings, find the "Reset" or "Backup & Reset" option, and follow the on-screen instructions. Make sure to remove any memory cards or SIM cards from the device before resetting it.
      Remove Your Accounts: Before selling your phone, it's important to remove any accounts associated with it. This includes your Google or Apple account, as well as any other accounts linked to apps and services on your device. This step helps prevent the new owner from accessing your accounts and personal information.
      Disable Factory Reset Protection (FRP): If your Android device has Factory Reset Protection (FRP) enabled, make sure to disable it before performing the factory reset. FRP is a security feature that requires the previous Google account credentials to set up the device after a reset. You can disable FRP in the device's settings under "Security" or "Accounts."
      By following these steps, you can protect your data and privacy while ensuring that your used phone is ready for a new owner. It's essential to take these precautions to avoid any potential issues and provide a positive experience for the buyer.

                  Repair Feature Details

                    Before You Begin:
                    Check Warranty: Determine if your electronic device is still under warranty. If it is, contact the manufacturer or authorized service center to avoid any out-of-pocket expenses.
                    Back Up Data: Backup any important data or files from your device to ensure you don't lose any information during the repair process.
                    Remove Accessories: Remove any accessories such as cases, chargers, SIM cards, or memory cards from the device.
                    After You Begin:
                    Introduction: Our mobile app provides a seamless way for customers to initiate and track mail-in repairs for their devices. Whether you have a smartphone, tablet, or other electronic device, follow these step-by-step instructions to utilize our mail-in repair service via the mobile app:
                    Step 1: Download and Install the App
                    1.1. Download and install our mobile app from your device's app store.
                    1.2. Log in to the app using your existing account credentials or conveniently sign in with your Google or Apple account.
                    Step 2: Initiate the Repair Request
                    2.1. From the home screen, tap the Repair Button within the relevant category.
                    2.2. Select the Brand and Model of your device from the available options.
                    Step 3: Describe the Repair
                    3.1. Users will be prompted to provide details about the type of repair needed and the current state of their devices. Answer these questions accurately to help our technicians understand the issue.
                    Step 4: Verify Device Information
                    4.1. For verification purposes, users will need to provide the IMEI Number and Serial Number of their devices. This ensures a secure and accurate repair process.
                    Step 5: Choose Repair Method
                    5.1. Users have the option to either "Ship In For Repair" to our ebin repair center or explore other available repair options.
                    Step 6: Ship In for Repair
                    6.1. If users choose to "Ship In For Repair," they should carefully package their device as instructed in the app and follow the shipping label generation process.
                    6.2. Drop off the package at their nearest postal service or courier drop-off location, keeping track of the shipment.
                    Step 7: Explore Local Repair Shops
                    7.1 Users interested in local repair options will be prompted with a list of our ebin Certified* repair shops in their area.
                    Step 8: Book an Appointment
                    8.1. After selecting a local repair shop, users can proceed with the booking process for their appointment.
                    Step 9: Confirm Reservation
                    9.1. Users will receive confirmation of their reservation for the upcoming appointment at one of our ebin Certified* repair shops.
                    Conclusion:
                    Our mobile app simplifies the process of initiating and tracking mail-in repairs or finding a nearby ebin Certified* repair shop. Whether you prefer the convenience of mail-in repair or the expertise of local technicians, our app ensures a hassle-free experience. If users have any questions or need assistance during any part of the process, our customer support team is readily available through the app to provide help and guidance.
