Guidelines for Affordable Screen Quality for Repair.

LCD Screen Quality

Affordable Series. When opting for a cost-effective screen replacement for a cracked display, we offer an LCD alternative instead of OLED. Despite this substitution, our LCD option still delivers vibrant colors at an affordable price point. Moreover, LCD panels typically boast a longer lifespan and are less susceptible to burn-in compared to OLED screens. However, it's important to note that LCD screens often exhibit lower contrast ratios compared to OLED screens, which can result in less deep blacks.
