What ebin does

A better way to repair or sell your device

Bring In for Repair

Local certified shops: ebin connects you with trusted local repair shops, making it easy to bring in your device for fast, reliable service whenever you need it.

All the common repairs: ebin covers screen replacement, battery replacement, back glass replacement, and more — most repairs are done same day, with others taking just a bit longer.

Skilled technicians: Your device is handled by experienced technicians who use proper tools and proven repair methods for safe, high-quality results.

Reliable updates: Partner shops provide clear updates and smooth communication from drop-off to pick-up.

Customer protection: If an issue ever occurs, ebin is here to support you — partner shops are committed to reworks or refunds when appropriate.

Ship In for Repair

All the common repairs: Repair times vary depending on the service needed, but the entire process — from shipping your device to receiving it back — can take 7 days or longer, including shipping and repair time.

Ship-in service: Ship your device to our facility and our technicians will diagnose, repair, and return it with care.

Free shipping: When you place a ship in for repair order, ebin provides a free shipping label.

Reliable updates: You can see every update of the repair on the app.

Sell

Convenient selling: Sell your phone or device easily through our online platform — no store visits or meetups, just a simple process from home.

Instant offer: Receive a fast, accurate offer in seconds based on your device's condition and market value — no negotiating.

Free shipping: When you place a sell order, ebin provides a free shipping label.

No meetups: Avoid unsafe or stressful in-person exchanges — your entire selling experience is handled online.

Fast payout: Once your device is delivered, inspected, and verified, payment is released quickly via Zelle or Venmo.
