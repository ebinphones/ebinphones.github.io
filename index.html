ebin Privacy Policy

This policy describes the information we process to support ebin.

What information do we collect?

  • Information you provide. When you add a phone to be sold through ebin, the phone's data that you provide (Model, Condition, Storage Capacity, Serial Number, etc.) is being used to estimate a price for the phone as well as help us determine whether the phone is legitimate. This includes the camera functionality and uploaded screenshot, which we also use to determine a legitimate phone. Your address information and Zelle® information is solely used to ship you a kit and send funds to your bank account, respectively.
  • Information from your Google account. Since we use your Google account for authentication purposes, your Google email address and UID is provided to us. We use your email address to email your information regarding your orders, and your UID to match orders and cancellation requests.

How do we use this information?

Your Google account is used to attribute the information that you input with your account. Information relating to phones that are inputted are only used to process that specific order. Unless your order is not accepted, your address information will be used to ship you a kit to mail your phone to us. Your shipping information will be provided to a third party shipping company for this purpose. Your Zelle® information will only be used to send funds to your bank account.

How can I manage or delete information about me?

Your login information is connected to your Google account, so for any questions or concerns relating to that please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-US. If you wish to manage any of the data relating to the information you provided us, feel free to contact us at ebinphones@gmail.com.
